Chinchabulous facts

Soft Fur: Chinchillas have super soft, dense fur to stay warm, with each follicle producing 50+ hairs.

Dust Baths Only: They clean themselves with dust, not water, to keep their fur from matting or getting infected.

Great Jumpers: Chinchillas can leap up to 6 feet high, which helps them escape predators.

Long Lives: They can live 15-20 years in captivity, making them some of the longest-living rodents.
